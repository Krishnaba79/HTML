My Dream Travel Bucket List

Paris, France

Visit the Eiffel Tower and enjoy fresh croissants by the Seine.

Kyoto, Japan

Experience cherry blossom season and explore ancient temples.

Santorini, Greece

Watch the sunset over white-washed houses with blue domes.

New York City, USA

See Times Square, Central Park, and the Statue of Liberty.

Bali, Indonesia

Relax on the beach, visit rice terraces, and enjoy peaceful temples.
