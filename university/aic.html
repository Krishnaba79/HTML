Promoted by Gujarat's oldest and the largest University, Gujarat University and established under the aegis of Atal Innovation Mission (NITI Aayog), AIC GUSEC is an agent of transformation and empowerment by indetifying and supporting startup ideas with impact. It offers a collaborative , no-cost incubation program to for-profit social enterprises and works extensively with public governance bodies, international developmental organisations, non-profit organisations, impact invesors and other cicil-society stakeholders.
